PROOF OF HUMAN WORK — CONFLICT OF INTEREST POLICY

Policy on conflicts of interest and disclosure

CONFLICT OF INTEREST POLICY

Status: Draft — Pending formal adoption

A PDF version will be available upon formal adoption by the Board of Directors.

1. Definition of Conflict of Interest

A conflict of interest exists when a Foundation member, director, or officer has a personal, financial, or professional interest that could influence their decisions or actions on behalf of the Foundation.

2. Disclosure Requirements

Board Members and Officers:

  • Annual disclosure of financial interests
  • Immediate disclosure of new conflicts
  • Disclosure of relationships with potential grant recipients
  • Disclosure of commercial interests in PoHW-related technologies

Technical Advisory Committee:

  • Disclosure of commercial affiliations
  • Disclosure of competing protocol interests
  • Disclosure of financial relationships

3. Prohibited Activities

  • Self-dealing or personal enrichment
  • Using Foundation position for private gain
  • Favoring personal or business interests
  • Accepting gifts that could influence decisions

4. Recusal Procedures

When a conflict exists:

  • Member must recuse themselves from related decisions
  • Recusal must be documented in meeting minutes
  • Alternative decision-makers identified
  • Public disclosure of recusal (where appropriate)

5. Grant Review Conflicts

  • Reviewers cannot evaluate their own proposals
  • Reviewers cannot evaluate proposals from close associates
  • Reviewers with financial interests must recuse
  • Alternative reviewers assigned when conflicts exist

6. Commercial Use

  • Foundation members may use PoHW commercially
  • Must disclose commercial interests
  • Cannot use Foundation position to gain commercial advantage
  • Foundation does not endorse commercial products

7. Enforcement

  • Violations reviewed by Ethics Review Board
  • Corrective actions may include removal
  • Public disclosure of violations (where appropriate)
  • Appeal process available

DISCLOSURE FORMS

Foundation members must complete annual conflict of interest disclosure forms.

Contact: ethics@proofofhumanwork.org for disclosure forms
