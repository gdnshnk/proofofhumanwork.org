FOUNDATION CHARTER

Status: Draft — Pending formal adoption

A PDF version will be available upon formal adoption by the Board of Directors.

Preamble

The PoHW Foundation is established to preserve, steward, and advance the Proof of Human Work protocol as a public good for the benefit of humanity in the synthetic media era.

Article I: Purpose

The Foundation exists to:

  • Maintain and evolve the PoHW protocol specification
  • Ensure long-term viability and durability of the protocol
  • Preserve ethical principles of privacy, openness, and accessibility
  • Support research and development in human authorship verification
  • Facilitate community participation and governance

Article II: Governance Structure

  • Board of Directors: Strategic oversight and decision-making
  • Technical Advisory Committee: Protocol review and standards
  • Ethics Review Board: Ethical oversight and dispute resolution
  • Community Representatives: Community voice and feedback

Article III: Core Principles

  • Openness: Open source, open standards, open participation
  • Privacy: Anonymity and privacy as fundamental rights
  • Durability: Commitment to 100-year continuity
  • Transparency: Public documentation and verifiable actions
  • Ethics: Verification as a civic right, not a commodity

Article IV: Protocol Stewardship

The Foundation is responsible for:

  • Maintaining the protocol specification
  • Ensuring backward compatibility
  • Managing protocol upgrades and improvements
  • Preserving cryptographic integrity

Article V: Financial Management

  • Non-profit operation
  • Public financial reporting
  • Transparent budget allocation
  • Donor disclosure (with consent)

Article VI: Amendment Process

This charter may be amended by:

  • Board of Directors approval (majority vote)
  • Community consultation period (30 days)
  • Public disclosure of changes
