FOUNDATION BYLAWS

Status: Draft — Pending formal adoption

A PDF version will be available upon formal adoption by the Board of Directors.

Section 1: Board of Directors

Composition:

  • Minimum 5 members, maximum 11 members
  • Staggered terms (3 years)
  • Diversity requirements: technical, ethical, community representation

Powers and Duties:

  • Strategic direction and policy decisions
  • Budget approval and financial oversight
  • Executive officer appointment
  • Protocol change approval (major changes)

Meetings:

  • Quarterly regular meetings
  • Special meetings as needed
  • Public minutes published within 30 days

Section 2: Technical Advisory Committee

  • Composed of protocol experts and researchers
  • Reviews technical proposals and protocol changes
  • Provides security assessments
  • Ensures standards compliance

Section 3: Ethics Review Board

  • Independent ethical oversight
  • Dispute resolution
  • Policy guidance on ethical matters
  • Community complaint handling

Section 4: Decision-Making Process

Protocol Changes:

  • Technical Advisory Committee review
  • Community consultation (30-60 days)
  • Board approval (majority vote)
  • Public disclosure and implementation

Grant Awards:

  • Technical evaluation
  • Ethics review (if applicable)
  • Board approval for large grants
  • Public disclosure (with recipient consent)

Section 5: Financial Procedures

  • Annual budget approval by Board
  • Quarterly financial reports
  • Annual audit (for grants over threshold)
  • Public financial transparency

Section 6: Transparency Requirements

  • All decisions publicly documented
  • Meeting minutes published
  • Financial reports disclosed
  • Governance logs maintained
