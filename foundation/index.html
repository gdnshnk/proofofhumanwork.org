PROOF OF HUMAN WORK — FOUNDATION
─────────────────────────────────

MISSION
───────

The PoHW Foundation preserves the ethical permanence and
long-term viability of the Proof of Human Work protocol.

We steward the protocol as a public good, ensuring it remains
open, verifiable, and durable for the next century.

VALUES
──────

1. ETHICAL GOVERNANCE
   Verification as a civic right, not a commodity.

2. TRANSPARENCY
   All decisions and actions are publicly verifiable.

3. DURABILITY
   Commitment to 100-year continuity of the protocol.

4. PRIVACY
   Anonymity and privacy are fundamental rights.

5. OPENNESS
   Open source, open standards, open participation.

RESPONSIBILITIES
────────────────

1. Protocol Stewardship
   - Maintain protocol specification
   - Ensure long-term viability
   - Preserve ethical principles

2. Attestor Accreditation
   - Review attestor applications
   - Conduct periodic audits
   - Maintain attestor registry

3. Dispute Resolution
   - Address governance disputes
   - Resolve technical conflicts
   - Ensure fair process

4. Transparency
   - Publish governance logs
   - Maintain public records
   - Enable community oversight

5. Community Engagement
   - Facilitate participation
   - Support contributors
   - Build ecosystem
