MISSION

The PoHW Foundation preserves the ethical permanence and long-term viability of the Proof of Human Work protocol.

We steward the protocol as a public good, ensuring it remains open, verifiable, and durable for the next century.

VALUES

1. ETHICAL GOVERNANCE

Verification as a civic right, not a commodity.

2. TRANSPARENCY

All decisions and actions are publicly verifiable.

3. DURABILITY

Commitment to 100-year continuity of the protocol.

4. PRIVACY

Anonymity and privacy are fundamental rights.

5. OPENNESS

Open source, open standards, open participation.
